Earthquake Data Dashboard

Welcome to the United States Geological Survey! The USGS is responsible for providing scientific data about natural hazards, the health of our ecosystems and environment; and the impacts of climate and land-use change. Their scientists develop new methods and tools to supply timely, relevant, and useful information about the Earth and its processes. The USGS provides earthquake data in a number of different formats, updated every 5 minutes.
